Things to Try

  • Run with defaults. Blue crawls, Green converges smoothly, Red overshoots. This is the core lesson.
  • Drag Red's lr even higher until it diverges. Where's the threshold?
  • Make Slow and Good match. Can you find a "slow" lr that still converges in 60 steps?
  • Look at the Sweet Spot chart (right). There's a valley of good learning rates. Too low = loss stays high (not enough steps). Too high = loss explodes.
  • Click different start positions. The best lr changes depending on where you start — closer to the minimum needs a smaller lr.
