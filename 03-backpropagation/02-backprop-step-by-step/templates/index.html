Things to Try

  • Click Forward, then Backward, then Update. This is one complete training step, decomposed. Watch the network diagram change at each phase.
  • Repeat the cycle 5-10 times. Watch the loss shrink and prediction approach the target. The network is learning!
  • Click "Train x50" to see rapid training. The prediction should converge close to the target.
  • Look at the gradient values. Notice how gradients get SMALLER as they flow back to earlier layers — this is the vanishing gradient problem in miniature.
  • Change target to 0 and train again. The network learns to output low values instead.
  • Compare the hidden layer gradients (dL/dW1) to output gradients (dL/dW2). The hidden gradients have passed through MORE multiplications, so they tend to be smaller.
